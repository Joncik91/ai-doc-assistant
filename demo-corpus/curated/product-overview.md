# Product overview

AI Document Assistant is a portfolio demo that shows a secure, cited RAG flow.

## What it does

- Ingests documents into a local registry
- Chunks and indexes the content for retrieval
- Answers questions with citations from the selected sources
- Surfaces guardrails, audit history, and runtime statistics for the operator

## Why it exists

The goal is to demonstrate disciplined AI product engineering: a swappable
provider layer, local retrieval, explicit safety controls, and a clear operator
experience.
